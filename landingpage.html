The Project

The Problem

Modern financial markets are defined by a significant information and technology asymmetry. Retail traders are often confined to lagging technical analysis, while institutions deploy sophisticated models and analyze market microstructure in real-time.

The Solution

Wekeza is a full-stack AI trading platform engineered to democratize access to institutional-grade strategies. It uses a multi-agent microservices architecture where specialized software agents collaborate to analyze data, manage risk, and execute trades.

Key Features

Multi-Agent Architecture

A collection of 12 independent, specialized services (agents) that collaborate to analyze market data and execute trades.

Tensor Analysis

Transforms raw market data into rich, multi-dimensional tensors for deep microstructure analysis.

Risk Management

Real-time Value at Risk (VaR) monitoring and automated stop-loss triggers to preserve capital.

NLP & Sentiment

Utilizes FinBERT to process real-time news feeds and earnings calls for sentiment analysis.
